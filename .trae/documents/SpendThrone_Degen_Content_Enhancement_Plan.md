## 2. Research Foundation

### 2.1 Academic Backing
- **Thorstein Veblen's Conspicuous Consumption Theory**: Individuals use luxury goods to signal wealth and social status
- **Status Signaling Psychology**: Economic inequality motivates status consumption behaviors
- **Digital Status Phenomena**: Modern platforms like pump.fun and highscore.money demonstrate willingness to pay for digital recognition

### 2.2 Platform Analysis
- **Pump.fun**: Solana-based meme coin creation platform with bonding curve model, appeals to users seeking rapid gains and social trading
- **Highscore.money**: Pay-to-play leaderboard where higher payments = higher rankings, pure status symbol game
- **SpendThrone Position**: Satirical commentary combining both concepts with permanent deposits for eternal digital status
