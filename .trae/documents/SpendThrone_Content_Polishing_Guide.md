# SpendThrone Content Polishing & Improvement Guide

## 1. Product Overview

SpendThrone is a Solana-based Web3 DeFi leaderboard application that allows users to deposit cryptocurrency (automatically converted to USDC via Jupiter API) to compete for ranking positions. The platform operates as a permanent deposit system with no withdrawals, focusing purely on status competition and blockchain-verified achievements.
